Soccer

is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. The game is played with a 
round ball
that players aim to move by kicking, passing, and dribbling. Goals are scored by getting the ball into the opposing team's net using any part of the body except the hands or arms, except for the goalkeeper who can use their hands within the penalty area. The defending and goalkeeping team tries to stop the attacking side from scoring and
win possession
of the ball.
